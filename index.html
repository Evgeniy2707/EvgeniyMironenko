Barcelona Football Club

Barcelona is a football club from Barcelona, ​​Catalonia, Spain. Plays in La Liga , the highest division of the Spanish football league system.

Emblem

The emblem takes the shape of a “boiler”, divided into three quarters. The top two have the ensign of the city of Barcelona, ​​the Cross of St. George and the ensign of Catalonia. The lower part of the skinned ball has the appearance of the first half of the 20th century on a background of blue-garnet smudge - the colors of the club. At the center of the shield on a golden smoothie, which represents the upper and lower parts of the coat of arms, the initials of the club FCB are inscribed
